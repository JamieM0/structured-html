Partially Automated

Steps to Full Automation

  1. Ingredient sourcing robotics (e.g., automated flour delivery systems)
  2. AI-controlled dough preparation with humidity/temperature sensors
  3. Robotic shaping and proofing chambers
  4. Autonomous baking with self-cleaning ovens
  5. Packaging and distribution via conveyor systems
